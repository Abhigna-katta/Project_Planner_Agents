## 1. Project Overview
# Project: Smart Agri Bot E-commerce Website
### Overview
This project involves building an attractive and functional e-commerce website to showcase and sell agriculture robots. A primary focus will be on the "Smart Agri Bot," specifically highlighting its benefits for chili farmers, with the site supporting product listings, secure transactions, and an engaging user experience.

### Key Deliverables
- Fully functional, attractive, and responsive e-commerce website.
- Detailed product pages for "Smart Agri Bot" and other agriculture robots.
- Secure shopping cart and checkout process with payment gateway integration.
- Admin panel for product, order, and user management.
- Comprehensive documentation for users and administrators.

## 5. Task-Level Risks

### TASK-005: Gather and Document Functional Requirements
- **Risk:** Incomplete or ambiguous requirements due to lack of detailed input from stakeholders, especially regarding niche features for "chili farmers."
- **Probability:** 4/5
- **Impact:** 4/5
- **Risk Score:** 16 (Critical)
- **Mitigation:** Schedule dedicated workshops with actual chili farmers or their representatives. Use visual aids and prototyping during requirement gathering.
- **Contingency:** Allocate buffer time in design/development sprints for rework based on clarifying requirements.

### TASK-007: Create Wireframes and User Flows
- **Risk:** Design iterations take longer than expected due to unclear user experience expectations or conflicting stakeholder feedback.
- **Probability:** 3/5
- **Impact:** 3/5
- **Risk Score:** 9 (High)
- **Mitigation:** Establish clear design principles upfront. Limit the number of review cycles per design stage.
- **Contingency:** Prioritize core user flows for initial wireframing; defer complex flows if delays occur.

### TASK-008: Develop UI/UX Mockups and Prototypes
- **Risk:** Significant delays in UI/UX development or rejection by stakeholders due to misalignment with brand guidelines or usability expectations.
- **Probability:** 4/5
- **Impact:** 4/5
- **Risk Score:** 16 (Critical)
- **Mitigation:** Conduct interim reviews with key stakeholders. Establish a clear brand guide and design system early.
- **Contingency:** Allocate extra design resources or extend the design sprint.

### TASK-009: Get Design Approval from Stakeholders
- **Risk:** Prolonged approval cycles due to stakeholder unavailability or difficulty reaching consensus.
- **Probability:** 4/5
- **Impact:** 3/5
- **Risk Score:** 12 (High)
- **Mitigation:** Schedule all review meetings in advance and ensure all decision-makers are present. Provide clear documentation for review.
- **Contingency:** Escalate to project sponsor for mediation if consensus cannot be reached within a defined timeframe.

### TASK-010: Define System Architecture
- **Risk:** Technical challenges or performance bottlenecks identified late in development due to incorrect architectural choices.
- **Probability:** 3/5
- **Impact:** 5/5
- **Risk Score:** 15 (Critical)
- **Mitigation:** Conduct thorough architecture review with experienced architects. Perform proof-of-concept (POC) for critical components/technologies.
- **Contingency:** Rearchitect critical modules if severe issues arise; this would cause significant delays and cost increases.

- **Risk:** Unexpected complexities or cost overruns during cloud infrastructure setup or configuration.
- **Probability:** 3/5
- **Impact:** 4/5
- **Risk Score:** 12 (High)
- **Mitigation:** Involve a cloud architect early. Use infrastructure-as-code (IaC) for consistent deployments.
- **Contingency:** Re-evaluate cloud provider or service options if costs become prohibitive or technical roadblocks are encountered.
